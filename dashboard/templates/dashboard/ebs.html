Amazon Aurora is a MySQL and PostgreSQL-compatible relational database built for the cloud. It combines the performance and availability of high-end commercial databases with the simplicity and cost-effectiveness of open-source databases.

üè¢ Real-World Use Case

A large e-commerce site uses Aurora to manage its customer orders and inventory. Thanks to Aurora's high availability and replication, the company ensures seamless customer experience even during sales peaks.

üîß How It Works
  1. Create an Aurora DB cluster with MySQL or PostgreSQL compatibility.

  3. Use automated backups and snapshots for recovery.
  4. Enable Multi-AZ deployments for high availability.
  5. Scale read and write capacity as needed.
