üí∏ EC2 Pricing Models
  • On-Demand: Pay per hour/second with no long-term commitment.
  • Reserved: Save up to 72% with 1 or 3-year usage commitment.
  • Spot: Get unused capacity at discounts up to 90%.
  • Dedicated Hosts: Physical servers for compliance-heavy workloads.
  • Capacity Reservation: Reserve capacity for spikes in demand.
